# Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)
**Dataset:** Netflix Titles Dataset
**Tools:** Python, Pandas, Matplotlib, Seaborn

## Problem Statement
Identify trends and patterns in Netflix content library.

## Analysis Performed
- Summary statistics and missing value analysis
- Movies vs TV Shows distribution
- Top 10 content producing countries
- Content addition trend over the years
- Top 10 most popular genres

## Key Insights
- USA is the top content producing country
- Movies dominate over TV Shows on Netflix
- International Movies and Dramas are the most popular genres
- Netflix saw peak content addition around 2019-2020
